Mission Statement

The state of technology is promising, yet poses concerns. There are more opportunities for innovation than ever before. As usual, with great power comes great responsibility. People are becoming increasingly dependent on unreliable tech. Technology has become wasteful, hard to maintain, and tech companies lack transparent practices. My goal is to use tech to simplify life, without complicating it.
